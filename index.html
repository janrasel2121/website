What makes us different?

With our facilities, it is easy to feel safe and cozy in our cafe. Having snacks, food, desserts, drinks, and even free wifi to suit your needs. Whether it be a quick drink or an outing with friends.

The staff are trained to take any request in drinks and in your service. Our staff are as approachable as can be and are trained to help the customers whenever needed.

MATCHA

Made from finely ground green tea leaves, matcha adds a vibrant green color and a slightly earthy, grassy flavor to your coffee.

02

CHOCOLATE

While not a traditional coffee drink, hot chocolate is a beloved option for chocolate lovers. Made with steamed milk and melted chocolate, it's a creamy and comforting beverage that's perfect for warming up on chilly days.

03

SPANISH LATTE

This traditional Spanish coffee drink is essentially a latte made with equal parts espresso and steamed milk. Simple yet satisfying, it's a staple of Spanish coffee culture.

04

CARAMEL MACCHIATO

A popular coffee beverage made with espresso, steamed milk, and caramel syrup, often topped with a swirl of caramel drizzle.
